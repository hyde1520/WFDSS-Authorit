6.1 Release New Functionality and Enhancements

6.1 Release will be deployed on Training 5/14/2018 and to Production on 5/16/2018

New Functionality and Enhancements

  • Uploading Images. You can upload images from almost any screen using the Upload images menu option to the left, or from the default decision editor when you insert comments. This makes images easy to upload when you plan your decision, or are finalizing your decision.
  • Incident Groups.
    • You can share group ownership among one or more individuals.
    • You can download incident decisions from the Incident Groups > View Incidentspage. This generates a zip file with the selected decisions.
  • Incident Objectives. You can prioritize incident objectives by selecting an objective and clicking the Move Up or Move Down button at the bottom of the screen.
  • Decisions. When a is submitted for review and or approval, the owner approver and reviewers all receive email notification when the decision becomes reviewable.

  • Re-enabling User Accounts. Disabled users will have their roles displayed on the User Roles page so when re-enabled, the former roles are known.
