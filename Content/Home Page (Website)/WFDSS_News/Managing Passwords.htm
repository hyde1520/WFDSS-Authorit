Managing Passwords

Your WFDSS user name and password allows you to access the WFDSS application. Users must change their passwords every 60 days. WFDSS sends 10-day and 3-day warning email alerts to help you avoid account lockout. If you fail to change your password prior to expiration, your account is locked.

When the new password does not meet all requirements, a warning displays and you must enter a new password that meets the criteria. Minimum password age is 24 hours.

You need to change or reset your password, through the WFDSS application or the Help Desk when you:

  • Successfully submit a Request User Account form. You receive a system generated email that contains an access key link. Click the link so you can set your password and security questions. After this is complete, you can login to WFDSS for the first time.
  • Forget your password. You can reset your password or call the HelpDesk.
  • Enter an invalid password more than five times. You must contact the HelpDesk to reset your password.
  • Do not access the system for more than 60 days. You must contact the HelpDesk to reset your password.
  • Fail to change your password in the 60-day period. You must contact the HelpDesk to reset your password.
