Setting or Resetting Your Password

Your WFDSS user name and password allows you to access the WFDSS application. Users must change their passwords every 60 days.

WFDSS users need to set/reset their passwords when they:

  • Successfully submit a Request User Account form and obtain a WFDSS user account.
  • Forget a password. You can reset your password or call the HelpDesk.
  • Enter an invalid password more than five times (the account is locked.) You must contact the HelpDesk to reset your password.
  • Do not access the system for more than 60 days. You must contact the HelpDesk to reset your password.
  • Fail to change your password in a 60-day period. You must contact the HelpDesk to reset your password.
