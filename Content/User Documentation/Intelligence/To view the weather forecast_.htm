To view the weather forecast:

  1. Choose Intelligence tab > Info sub-tab. The Information page appears in the left pane.
  2. Select the Information icon and place your cursor on the map where you want to know the forecast.
  3. Click Weather Forecast. The current forecast for the selected area appears.
  4. Review the information in light of current and expected fire conditions.

The weather data are automatically included in the decision content tree for the incident (Decision > Assessment > Weather).
