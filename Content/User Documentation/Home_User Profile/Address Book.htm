Address Book

The address book in WFDSS allows you to easily find other WFDSS users, and to create groups based on your area of responsibility, geographic area, or other criteria. You can also use the address book to search for a specific person.

In this section, you will find the following tasks:

  • Filtering Addresses
  • Setting Up Your Personal Address Book
  • Creating Groups
  • Editing Groups
  • Removing Groups or Users from your Personal Address Book
  • Updating Your Contact Information
