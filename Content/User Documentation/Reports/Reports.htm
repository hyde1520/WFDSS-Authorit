Reports

WFDSS enables you to create three types of reports for your incidents.

  • Custom Reports
  • Management Action Point Reports
  • Delegation of Authority Reports

These reports are useful for conducting inbriefs and other meetings, as well as for preparing after action reviews and post-fire reclamation plans. Reports are developed by pulling information from your WFDSS documentation such as decision content, maps, tables and images and adding text to tie it all together in a publishable report.

You can perform the following tasks:

  • Create a report
  • Edit a report
  • Publish a report
  • Download a report

If you are an Incident Owner, Data Manager, GA Editor, or National Editor, you can also create and manage Report Templates.
