Viewing Custom Reports

All WFDSS users can view reports that have been published for incidents, as well as download published reports as .PDF or .HTML files. Only WFDSS users assigned Incident Owner or Editor privileges for a particular incident can view report content that is pending and not yet published. Users with Incident Owner and Editor privileges can also edit and publish reports. They can also create .PDF drafts for downloading, printing, and sharing.
