Data Management Overview

Data Management is the oversight of textual and spatial Land, Resource and Fire management plan-related data for an administrative unit. The WFDSS Data Manager role is responsible for creating, managing, and uploading the text and associated spatial data related to these plans for a unit. WFDSS data management includes:

  • Determining a planning process for their unit.
  • Creating and managing Unit Shapes that include Fire Management Units or Strategic Objectives, Management Requirements, and Other Unit Shapes.
  • Creating text and associating shapes for Strategic Objectives and Management Requirements.
  • Viewing Unit Fire Planning map layers on the Unit Map.
  • Viewing Change History.
  • Creating, editing, and deleting Data Management Notes.
  • Exporting, Importing, or Deleting Planning Processes.
