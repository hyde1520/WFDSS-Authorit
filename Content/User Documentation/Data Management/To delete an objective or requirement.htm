To delete an objective or requirement

  1. From the Incident List, choose Data Management > Objectives. The Strategic Objectives page appears.
  2. Select the Strategic Objective or Management Requirement you want to delete. The buttons at the bottom of the list become active.

    Note: If Delete is not active after you select the objective or requirement, then that item has been previously activated and can no longer be deleted. If a Strategic Objective or Management Requirement has been activated, it will show an activation date in the Activated column.

  3. Click Delete. A message appears asking if you are sure you want to delete the item.
  4. Click OK. The Objectives/Requirements List reappears and the item is removed from the list.
