To query the Landscape Data:

  1. From the Incident List, select the incident for which you would like to query the landscape data.
  2. Click Situation Assessment. The Situation map appears.
  3. Click the Info tab, and the Information pane appears.
  4. Select the icon, and then left click in the fire area. A small yellow circle appears where you clicked, and landscape data for that point appears in the Information pane on the left.
  5. Click around the immediate fire area to determine in what fuel models the fires is currentlyburning.
