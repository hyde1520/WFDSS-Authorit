To view FMU lists for an incident:

  1. From the Incident list, select the incident > click View Information. The Edit Information page appears.
  2. From the left menu, choose FMU/SO List. The FMU/SO List page appears and the primary GA, agency and unit are listed.
  3. To view all of the FMUs/SOs associated with the incident, scroll down to the FMU/SOs for Incident section.
