To declare an incident out:

  2. Click View Information. The Edit Incident Information page appears.

  3. Verify that the incident information is accurate and complete.

    • Verify the Unique Fire Identifier, Incident Size, Responsible Agency and so on.
    • Choose Situation to view the Incident Situation Map and review shapes that are associated with the incident.
    • Choose Objectives to review the incident objectives associated with the incident.
    • Choose Course of Action to verify that the selected course of action is current.
    • Choose Cost to enter an estimated final cost.
    • Choose Decisions to ensure that any pending decisions have been published as appropriate.
    • Choose Periodic Assessments to verify that this information is current.
  4. On the Edit Incident Information page, scroll down to the Out Date field and enter the date and time that the incident was declared out.
  5. Click Save. A message appears asking if you are sure you want to declare the incident out.
  6. Click OK. The incident is now out and much of the incident information is no longer editable.
