To edit incident obj. shape associations:

  1. Choose the Incidents tab > select the incident > click Assess Situation. The map appears with the selected incident centered on the map.
  2. Choose Map tab > Incident layer > Incident Obj Shapes > select the shape you want to edit associations for. The selected shape appears on the map as a pink outline.
  3. On the map, click the shape label. The Incident Objectives/Requirements Associated with Shape page appears. All currently associated objectives/requirements are listed.
  4. Click Edit Shape Associations. The Associate Incident Objectives/Requirements to Shape page appears. The available incident objectives/requirements are listed in the table.
  5. Mark/unmark the checkboxes for each objective and requirement that you want to edit the shape association.
  6. Click Save Shape Association(s). A message appears at the top of the page indicating that your associations were saved.

You can edit the associations even after a decision is published. Incident obj. shapes appear pink on the map.
