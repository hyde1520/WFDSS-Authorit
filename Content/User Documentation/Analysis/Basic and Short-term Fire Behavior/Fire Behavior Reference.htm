Fire Behavior Reference

Fire behavior modeling is a complex activity that requires large amounts of data and contains many variables. The reference material provided here is intended to assist you in understanding these models and the concepts behind them.

The models currently available in WFDSS are:

  • Automated Basic Fire Behavior (BFB)
  • Automated Short Term Fire Behavior (STFB)
  • Near Term Fire Behavior (NTFB)
  • FSPro
