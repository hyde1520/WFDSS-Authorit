Analyst_Assisted Short Term Fire Behavior (STFB)

Fire Behavior Specialists (FBS) can modify the General Information parameters for Short Term Fire Behavior analyses that they are running. The following parameters can be modified in an Short-Term Fire Behavior Analysis:

  • Analysis Name.
  • Analysis Date
  • Hour
  • Conditioning Days
  • Burn Period
  • Number of (Burn) Periods
  • Foliar Moisture Content
  • Crown Fire Method
  • Spotting Probability
