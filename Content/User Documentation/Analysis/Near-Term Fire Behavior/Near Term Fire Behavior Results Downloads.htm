Near Term Fire Behavior Results Downloads

Near Term Fire Behavior outputs are available for download to view in other programs such as ArcMap and Google Earth. Files labeled as degrees, meters, or ASCII grids can be opened in ArcMap. Grayed-out downloads indicate the item was not used in the analysis and is therefore not available. KMZ Downloads can be opened in Google Earth. Available downloads include:

  • Arrival Time (degrees)
  • Arrival Time (meters)
  • Burn Periods (degrees)
  • Burn Periods (meters)
  • Ignition (degrees)
  • Ignition (meters)
  • Barrier (degrees)
  • Barrier (meters)
  • Arrival Time ASCII Grid
  • KMZ (color for each 3 hr interval)
  • KMZ (one color per burn period)
  • Analysis Input
