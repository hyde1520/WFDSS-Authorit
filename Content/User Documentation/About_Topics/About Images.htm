About Images

WFDSS users with the following roles and privileges can capture or upload images for use in an incident's decision:

  • Incident Owners and Editors,
  • Fire Behavior Specialists,
  • Dispatchers (must be assigned to the same geographic area as the incident), and
  • Geographic Area Editors (must be assigned to the same geographic area as the incident).

Captured and uploaded images are saved to the incident content, and incident owners and editors can add these to a pending decision. Images are accessible via the default or advanced Decision Editor, and these can only be accessed after a decision is created (but before it is published).

Note: The image description cannot be edited after the initial upload.

Captured images can also be turned into landscape-oriented reports at the time of image creation. Reports can be downloaded and shared as .pdfs, and edited and published as you would a decision.
