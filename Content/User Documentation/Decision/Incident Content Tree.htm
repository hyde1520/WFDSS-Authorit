Incident Content Tree

The incident content tree is located at the bottom left pane of the Advanced Decision Editor page and can be viewed by incident owners and editors when editing a pending decision.

It contains all information associated with the incident:

  • Incident Information and History: All the information found on the Edit Incident page, plus history of what's happened with the incident
  • Assessment: All Fire Weather and Planning Area information
  • Objectives: All Incident Objectives, Incident Requirements, Strategic Objectives, and Management Requirements included with all decision documents
  • Course of Action: All COAs that have been included with all decision documents
  • Relative Risk and Organization Assessment
  • Periodic Assessments: Decision History and Information
  • FSPro: Information, Results, ERC Classes, ERC Stream, Winds, Landscape, Values at Risk for all accepted analyses
  • Basic: Analysis Information, Station Information, and History
  • Short-Term Fire Behavior: Analysis Information, Station Information, History, and Values Inventory
  • Images: All saved and uploaded maps, photos, and miscellaneous images

The above information needs to be inserted into the Decision Content Tree to be included with a decision because not all analyses and images should be included with a particular decision. Carefully evaluate which incident information is appropriate for inclusion in the pending decision.
