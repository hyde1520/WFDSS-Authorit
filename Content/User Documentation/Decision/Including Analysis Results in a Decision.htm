Including Analysis Results in a Decision

Incident Owners and Editors can include fire behavior analysis results in a decision document. This is useful when results from fire behavior analyses are used to inform or support decision-making, relative risk assessment, courses of action, and/or rationale. The analysis must be "Accepted" by the Fire Behavior Specialist who completed the analysis before you can include it in a decision. 

You can include a graphic of the analysis results, as well as other user-entered analysis information and text outputs, such as the following:

  • Values at Risk
  • Values Inventory
  • Histograms
  • Station Information
  • Annotations
