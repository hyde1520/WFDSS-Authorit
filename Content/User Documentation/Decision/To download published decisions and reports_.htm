To download published decisions and reports:

  1. Navigate to your incident.
  2. Click View Information.
  3. Click the Decisions or Reports tab.
  4. Click the radio button next to the Published Decision or Report that you want to download for saving or printing.

  5. Click either PDF or HTML. The File Download box appears.

    • If selected PDF, open the PDF for viewing and/or printing, or save it to your hard drive.
    • If you selected an HTML file, save the zipped file and then extract its contents to your hard drive.
