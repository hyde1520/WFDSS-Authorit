Activation Date

In WFDSS, an item is activated on the date that a decision including the item is published. The item is deactivated when a decision is published that specifically excludes the item.

These activation dates appear automatically when the decision is published, and affect the following items:

  • decision summary
  • incident objectives (under incident requirements)
  • action items (under course of action)
  • management action points (under course of action)
