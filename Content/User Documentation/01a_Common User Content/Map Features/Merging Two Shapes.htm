Merging Two Shapes

Incident Owners and Editors can merge shapes for their incidents. You might want to merge shapes under the following circumstances:

  • to add to an existing fire perimeter
  • to expand your planning area based on new data
  • to expand the area covered by a set of objectives
  • to add area to a management action point
  • to include additional barriers in the barriers file
