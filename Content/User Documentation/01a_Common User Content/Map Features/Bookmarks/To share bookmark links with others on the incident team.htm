To share bookmark links with others on the incident team

  1. Click the Bookmarks icon directly above the map and the map tools.
  2. Highlight the bookmark you want to share and click Show Link. The link displays below the Add box.
  3. Highlight and copy the link and place it into an email. A message displays the you exported a bookmark
  4. You can share a bookmark from the bookmarks icon by clicking Share Link. You can share a link to any existing bookmark regardless of the map context you are currently on.
