Accessing the Fire Application Help Desk

If you cannot find an answer to your question in the WFDSS Help, you can call the elp Desk at 1-866-224-7677 (Toll Free), +1

616-323-1667, or navigate to https://iiahelpdesk.nwcg.gov/. Fire Applications Help Desk provides level 1 technical support for WFDSS.

If they cannot answer your question, they will move you up to level 2 technical support.
