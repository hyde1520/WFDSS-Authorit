Approval Process for Line Officers

The following bullets quickly summarize the help content with images, to assist Line Officers/Agency Administrators with decision approval process.

  • Your Reviewers and Approvers receive an e-mail with a link to WFDSS Login and then the Decision Page.
  • Figure 1: Example of Email with Decision Page
  • Reviewers and Approvers login to WFDSS.
  • Figure 2: Example of WFDSS Application Login Page
  • The link takes them to the "Pending Decision" for Approve Decision or Reject Decision. At this point, the Reviewers/Approvers can navigate through the decision document using the navigation arrows above the pending decision.
  • Figure 3: Example of Pending Decision Page
  • When Rejecting a decision, your Reviewer or Approver must provide a comment that explains why it was rejected. This will enable you to change the document to suit them.
  • Figure 4: Example of Decision Rejection Page
  • When approving a decision, your Approver (final Approver if there are multiple Approvers) must choose the days between periodic assessments. Give careful consideration to selecting the number of days. On active incidents, consider daily periodic assessments; inactive incidents can be longer intervals between assessments.
  • To receive an email reminder of the periodic assessment, the Approver checks the box next to "Send me an email reminder…" (as in the first image below). The checkbox will not be available to an Approver if the incident has only one Approver (as in the second image below). At least one Approver must receive the email.
  • Figure 5: Example of Pending Decision Approved Page
  • The Approver clicks Publish Decision. The Decision List page reappears, the document is listed as Published and locked. No additional edits or information can be added once the document is published.
  • Figure 6: Example of Publish Decision Page
  • To download the decision content, click either the PDF or HTML options. Either type can be savedto your hard drive (the HTML optionis savedas a Zip file and its contents must be extracted).

    Figure 7: Example of Download Decision Content Page

Note: To access the decision without clicking the emailed link, go to Incident List > select the incident > View Information > Decisions > select the pending decision > Review/Approve Decision.
