Release Notes - 6.4.1

6/26/2019 - Bug Fixes for Release 6.4.1

New Decision Support Number

  • The new National Fire Decision SupportCenter (NFDSC)number is 208-387-5253.

Re-enabling Disabled Accounts

  • WFDSS user accounts lock when not accessed in 60 days and require a password reset by the IIA HelpDesk (https://iiahelpdesk.nwcg.gov/ and +1 866-244-7677) to unlock. Locked accounts become disabled after 270 days of inactivity and require a Geographic Area Editor (GAE) to re-assign user role(s), and then a password reset by the IIA HelpDesk.

    You receive email notifications 30 days and 3 days prior to account disabling to allow time for a locked account password reset. When 270 days lapse and your account is disabled, you receive a final email letting you know your account was disabled. It will contain instructions on how to proceed.

Map Quality Improvements

  • Map quality has substantially improved for user accounts created after 2014. A bug introduced by a past release and unknowntothe WFDSS teamwas detected by a user andresolved by developers. User accounts impacted will notice improved viewing capabilities for analysis outputs, historic fire perimeters and other dynamically-rendereddata.
