Editing a Group

Incident Groups owners can:

  • Change the incident group name by typing over the existing name and clicking Save.
  • Change incident group ownership,
  • Change incident group type if you are the incident group owner by clicking the incident group type from private to public or visa versa.

Note: The Incident Groups Owner must be an owner of all incidents in a group to make a group public.

From the Incidents Groups list, you can:

  • View information for a selected incident by clicking View Information.
  • View the assessment map for a selected incident by clicking Assess Situation.
  • View analyses information for a selected incident by clicking View Analysis.
  • Permanently delete the selected incident from the group by clicking Remove Incident.
  • Click View Incident to review incident group information in Excel format by clicking the Excel icon to the left of the Incident Name column.
  • Click View Incident and click the hyperlink below the Current Decision columnto review decision status. If a published decision exists, the date is the hyperlink. If the first decision is pending, the hyperlink displays as "Pending." The column is blank for incidents without decisions.

You can also:

  • Add more incidents to the group.
  • View the group map.
  • Download group perimeters.
  • Generate a KMZ for the group.
