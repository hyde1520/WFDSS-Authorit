Unit ID

A code used within the interagency wildland fire community to uniquely identify a particular organizational unit within the government or a non-government organization recognized by NWCG as a wildland fire cooperator. The NWCG Data Standards use the term Point of Origin Responsible Agency Unit Identifier, but the field has been shortened to Unit ID on the WFDSS incident information page. It is one of three parts that comprises the Unique Fire Identifier.
