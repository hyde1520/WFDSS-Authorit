WFDSS jurisdicational unit at point of origin

The agency with administrative responsibilities related to an incident's location. This field displays one of three values:

  • Not Set means the Unit ID is not available from the FMA or SMA layer.
  • Set means the Unit ID is available from the FMA or SMA layer.
  • Unavailable means the Unit ID is not available from the FMA or SMA layer because the location is on State or Local land.
