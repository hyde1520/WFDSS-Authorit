To view NTFB KMZ animations

  1. Make sure you have Google Earth installed on your computer.
  2. After downloading the KMZ from a Near Term Fire Behavior simulation, open the file in Google Earth.
  3. Click the Time Slider Animation options in Google Earth. The animation steps through time to display the simulated fire growth over time, displaying Flame Length. Depending on user settings it may be necessary to use Google Earth Help to display the Time Slider Animation options as desired, such as looping and speed settings.
