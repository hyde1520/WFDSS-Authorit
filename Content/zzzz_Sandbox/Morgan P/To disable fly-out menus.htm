To disable fly-out menus

  1. Navigate to My Home > System Preferences.
  2. CheckEnhanced Accessibility in the Accessibility section.
  3. Select Save Preferences at the bottom of the page.

Keep in mind that turning on the Enhanced Accessibility will not only turn fly-out menus into static ones, it will also turn on other accessibility features required to meet 508 compliance, for example the Skip to Content feature that allows screen readers to skip to the primary field of interest on the page.
