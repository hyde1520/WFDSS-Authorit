Incident Quick Search

The Incident Quick Search feature allows you to quickly locate incidents by name and year. It is located on the Incidents tab within the Incident List Filter section.

To use the Incident Quick Search feature

  1. From the Incidents tab type the name of the incident into the Incident Name field.
  2. Type the year the incident started into the Incident Year field.
  3. Click Find Incidents.
  4. All the incidents that contain the search name entered for the indicated year will display.
