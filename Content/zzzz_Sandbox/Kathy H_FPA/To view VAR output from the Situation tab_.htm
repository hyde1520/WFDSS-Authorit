To view VAR output from the Situation tab:

  1. Navigate to the Situation tab for an incident.
  2. Expand the Analysis menu option tree from the left hand menu under the sub tab Map by clicking the plus button to the left of Analysis.
  3. Expand the FSPro Output option tree by clicking the plus(+) button to the left of FSPro Output.
  4. Under Show Information, select the drop-down black arrow button to the right of the desired FSPro analysis and click Values at Risk.
