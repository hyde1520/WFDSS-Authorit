Decision Content: Benefits

The Benefits section of a decision contains a Benefits slider bar to show the amount of benefit expected from a fire and an associated comment box to document and describe the natural resource, intrinsic, and/or other benefits of the fire. 

These tools are available from the Benefits vertical tab if using the default decision editor, or they can be accessed from the Assessment tab of the incident perspective if you plan to use the advanced decision editor for decision editing.

Use of the Benefits slider bar and comment box are optional. A graphic of the slider is included in the decision if you choose.
