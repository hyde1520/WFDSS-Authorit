Generating KMZ Files from the Incident List

WFDSS users can generate KMZ files that contain information for all fires in the Incident List. Fire perimeters, completed and accepted analyses, Points of Interest, and items from the most recently published decision (planning areas, M.A.P.s and Incident Obj. shapes) can be included for selection in the KMZ.
