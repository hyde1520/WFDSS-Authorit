Creating Custom Reports

An incident owner or editor can create custom reports that are customized to meet the needs of the user. You can create and publish custom reports that include a customized selection of active, inactive and/or yet to be published content.

Publishing your report locks the report's content and name and both become permanent. Once a report is published, it can be downloaded and printed. You will not be able to publish a report that contains content that has not yet been published in a decision, but you can download a draft .PDF for viewing and/or sharing.

Improvements to the Reports function are scheduled for future WFDSS releases in 2014.
