To view or download Other Unit Shapes

  1. Navigate to a map display in WFDSS.

    • From the Data Management > select the Unit Map sub-tab.
    • From the Incident List > select the incident > click Assess Situation. The Situation Map appears.
    • From the AnalysisList> select a completed analysis > click View Results. The map for the selected analysis appears.
    • Choose the Intelligence tab. A map of your assigned Geographic Area appears.
  2. Choose the Map sub-tab if it’s not already selected. It is located to the left of the map display.
  3. Locate and expand the Unit Fire Planning map layers.
  4. Locate and expand the shape for the administrative unit you want to view Other Unit Shapes for. The administrative Unit Outline displays on the map.
  5. Expand the Other Unit Shapes map layers, and then locate and expand the categories of shapes you are interested in.
  6. Select the shape(s) you want to display. The map refreshes and displays the selected shape.

  7. When the shape doesn't appear on your screen:

    1. Click the down arrow to the right of the shape. The shape tools appear.
    2. Select the Pan tool. The map shifts to the shape on the map.
    3. Zoom out to see the analysis or incident in relationship to the selected shape.
  8. Once you are zoomed in, select the tool, and then click inside the shape. The Info tab replaces the Map tab on the left side of the screen.
  9. Select Feature Information and a window appears that contains shape information.
