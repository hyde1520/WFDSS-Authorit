Editing Associations for Management Requirement Shapes

Data Managers can change the set(s) of Management Requirement text associated with a Management Requirement shape from the Unit Map and the Data Management > Objectives sub-tab. You can only associate Management Requirement text with Management Requirement shapes that are not activated.
