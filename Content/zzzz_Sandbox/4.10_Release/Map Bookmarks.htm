Map Bookmarks

This My Home sub-tab displays the Bookmark Name and Location for all of your map bookmarks. Each entry under the Bookmark column is underlined in blue with blue text to indicate this is a hyperlink you can click to display the map page containing the bookmark. You can also delete one or more bookmarks by clicking Delete or Delete All. See Bookmarking Maps for more information about creating and working with map bookmarks.

To exit the Map Bookmarks sub-tab:

  • Click another sub-tab in My Home, or
  • If you clicked on a hyperlink from the Map Bookmarks sub-tab and are on another page of the application, click the My Home tab.
