My Incidents

This My Home sub-tab displays Incident Name, Discovery Date, Incident Status, Last Publication Date, Periodic Assessment Status, Decision Status, and your Role. My Incidents quickly allows users to review information about each incident where they are the Owner or Approver. This feature provides easy navigation for line officers. Each entry under the Incident Name, Last Publication Date, and Periodic Assessment Status columns are underlined in blue with blue text to indicate these are hyperlinks you can click to display relevant information for the column. The Role column displays your role or roles relative to the decision for this incident, not your user privileges in the WFDSS application. For example, click an Incident Name in the Incident Name column, and the application navigates to the Incident Information page for that incident.

Periodic Assessment Status can be:

  • Decision No Longer Valid. You cannot do another periodic assessment until a new decision is published.
  • page.Due on <date>. This displays when decision already published. The hyperlink takes you to the Periodic Assessment page.
  • Overdue. The hyperlink takes you to the Periodic Assessment page.

Decisions status can be:

  • Pending. If the decision is pending, there is a hyperlink to the decision page.
  • Published.
  • Reviewable. When you have Approver or Reviewer privileges the hyperlink takes you to the decision. You can approve the decision or return the decision for edits.

To exit the My Incidents sub-tab:

  • Click on another sub-tab in My Home, or
  • If you clicked on a hyperlink from the My Incidents sub-tab and are on another page of the application, click the My Home tab.
