My Activities

This My Home sub-tab displays recent activity over the past week, the Activity Status, Data Requested, and the Expiration Date. Each entry under the Activity Status is underlined in blue with blue text to indicate these are hyperlinks you can click to display relevant information for the column. You can also delete one or more activities by clicking Delete or Delete All

To exit the My Activities sub-tab:

  • Click another sub-tab in My Home, or
  • If you clicked on a hyperlink from the My Activities sub-tab and are on another page of the application, click the My Home tab.
