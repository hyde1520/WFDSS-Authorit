Adding Incident Notes

Incident Authors and Editors can enter incident notes for an incident at any time regardless of whether a decision has been created and/or is in the review process. Incident notes are not required and are not tied to a decision. Incident Notes can be used by Authors and Editors to document and record information about the incident separate from a decision, or before a decision is deemed necessary.
